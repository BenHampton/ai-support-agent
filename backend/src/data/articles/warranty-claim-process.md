## Warranty Coverage

| Customer Type | Warranty Period |
|---|---|
| Consumer products | 1 year from date of purchase |
| Enterprise hardware (ARK-R Series, NAS) | 3 years |

Coverage applies only to products purchased directly from Ark Systems or an authorized reseller and is non-transferable.

## What You Need to File a Claim

- **Proof of purchase** — order confirmation or invoice
- **Serial number** — found on the device label or in your account portal under Registered Products
- **Defect description** — brief summary of the issue

## How to File

1. Submit your claim through the Ark Systems Support portal
2. Our team validates eligibility and issues a case number within 1 business day
3. You will be contacted with repair options based on your tier and product type

## Repair Options

- **Depot service** — ship the device to Ark Systems; turnaround is typically 5–7 business days
- **On-site service** — available for Enterprise customers with active SLA entitlements; prioritized for server and storage hardware

Ark Systems covers parts, labor, and return shipping for defective units under warranty.

## What Voids the Warranty

- Physical damage
- Misuse or abuse
- Unauthorized modifications
