## Billing Cycles

- Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually depending on the plan selected at signup
- Invoices are generated on the first day of each billing period
- Available in your ArkCloud account portal under **Billing > Invoice History**
- Each invoice includes a breakdown by service (compute, storage, managed services) and usage overages
- All invoices reference a unique Invoice ID in the format `ARK-YYYY-XXXXXX`

## Plan Changes & Proration

| Change | When It Takes Effect | Billing Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Upgrade | Immediately | Prorated charge within 24 hours |
| Downgrade | Start of next billing cycle | Credit applied to next invoice |
| Cancellation (mid-cycle) | End of current cycle | No refund for unused portion (standard terms) |

Annual plan customers may be eligible for a partial refund on cancellation — contact billing support for details.

## Disputing a Charge

1. Locate the Invoice ID in your portal (`ARK-YYYY-XXXXXX`)
2. Navigate to **Billing > Dispute a Charge**
3. Include the Invoice ID, the specific line item in question, and a brief explanation

### By Customer Tier

- **Consumer / SMB** — resolve through the self-serve portal
- **Enterprise** — contact your named Customer Success Manager
- **VIP** — dedicated support pod responds within 1 business hour
