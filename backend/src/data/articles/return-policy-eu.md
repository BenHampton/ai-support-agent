## Statutory Right of Withdrawal

Under EU consumer protection law (Directive 2011/83/EU), all Ark Systems customers in the European Union have a statutory right of withdrawal of **14 days** from the date of delivery. This right:

- Applies to all distance and off-premises contracts
- Is valid regardless of the product's condition
- Supersedes any shorter Ark Systems product-specific policy
- Does not require a reason to exercise

## How to Exercise Your Right of Withdrawal

1. Notify Ark Systems in writing before the 14-day period expires
2. Submit a withdrawal request through your Ark Systems account portal or email eu-returns@arksystems.com
3. Once confirmed, you have a further 14 days to return the product

## Reimbursement

- Ark Systems will reimburse all payments received, including standard delivery charges
- Reimbursement is issued within 14 days of receiving the returned product or proof of shipment

## After the 14-Day Window

- EU customers may still be eligible for a warranty-based return if the product is defective
- All personal data collected during the return process is handled in accordance with our GDPR Privacy Policy
