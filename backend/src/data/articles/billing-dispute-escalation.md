## Consumer & SMB

1. Go to the self-serve **Billing Dispute portal** at arksystems.com/billing/dispute
2. Provide your Invoice ID (`ARK-YYYY-XXXXXX`), the disputed amount, and a brief explanation
3. Disputes must be submitted within **90 days** of the invoice date
4. Most disputes resolve within **5 business days**
5. If unresolved, the case is automatically escalated to the billing specialist team

## Enterprise

1. Contact your named **Customer Success Manager** directly
2. The CSM opens a priority case with the Ark Systems Finance team
3. Updates provided within **24 hours**
4. Disputes involving annual contract true-ups or usage overages may take up to **10 business days**

> CSMs are authorized to apply provisional credits up to $5,000 while a dispute is under review.

## VIP

- VIP customers must **never** be directed to self-serve billing tools
- All VIP billing issues are handled by the dedicated support pod and escalated to a human agent immediately
- The VIP pod has direct access to the Finance escalation queue and will engage the account executive if needed

| | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|
| VIP billing dispute | Within 1 business hour | Within 1 business day |
