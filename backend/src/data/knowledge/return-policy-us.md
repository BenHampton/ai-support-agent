## Eligibility

- 30-day return window for products purchased from arksystems.com or an authorized Ark Systems retail partner
- Product must be in its original, unopened condition or deemed defective by our support team
- Products purchased from third-party resellers are not eligible for a direct Ark Systems return

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Contact Ark Systems Support to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
2. Returns shipped without an RMA number will be refused
3. Once your RMA is issued, a prepaid shipping label will be provided if the return is due to a defect or error on our part
4. Customer-initiated returns for non-defective products require the customer to cover return shipping costs

## Refunds

- Processed within 5–10 business days of receiving the returned item at our warehouse
- Issued to the original payment method
- Items returned outside the 30-day window or without an RMA number are not eligible and will be shipped back at the customer's expense
