## Our Commitment

Ark Systems is a data controller fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for all customers in the EU and EEA. We process personal data only for specified, lawful purposes:

- Order fulfillment
- Support case management
- Product warranty administration
- Service improvement

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## Your Rights Under GDPR

- **Right of access** — obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Right to rectification** — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to erasure** ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data
- **Right to restrict processing** — limit how we use your data
- **Right to data portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Right to object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request through your Ark Systems account portal or contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@arksystems.com. We will respond to all verified requests within **30 days**.

## Data Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Transactional records | 7 years (EU accounting requirements) |
| Support case records | 3 years |
| Marketing consent records | Until consent withdrawn + 1 year |

All data is stored in EU-region infrastructure.

## Complaints

If you believe we have processed your data unlawfully, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority.
