## SLA Summary

| Tier | Response SLA | Support Hours | Named CSM |
|---|---|---|---|
| SMB | Next Business Day (NBD) | Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm local | No |
| Enterprise | 4 hours (P1/P2) | 24/7 for critical hardware | Yes |
| VIP | 1 hour | 24/7 dedicated pod | Yes (dedicated team) |

## SMB

- Next-Business-Day response for hardware issues
- Email and chat support with a 4-hour response window during business hours
- Handled by the general support queue

## Enterprise

- 4-hour response SLA for P1/P2 incidents
- 24/7 phone support for critical hardware failures
- Named Customer Success Manager for account health, renewals, and escalations
- Access to the Ark Systems Enterprise Support Portal for cases, firmware, and driver updates
- On-site hardware repair available within 24 hours in covered metro areas

## VIP

- 1-hour response SLA with a dedicated support pod
- VIP incidents are never routed to the general queue
- Dedicated Slack channel for real-time communication
- Proactive health checks and priority access to beta firmware
- Billing disputes must always be handled by the dedicated pod and escalated to a human agent — never resolved through self-serve tooling
